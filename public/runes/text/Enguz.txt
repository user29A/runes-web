The alternate symbol for this rune is much like the sigil of Azazel. This rune is the male counterpart to Berkano. Represents the God “Ing.” Ingwaz is the rune in which power is stored. Converts active power into potential power. Can deprive a man of his masculinity or anyone, male or female, of their life force. Like a crystal, this rune stores energy until it is needed. It is a magickal reserve. Stone is ivory.
